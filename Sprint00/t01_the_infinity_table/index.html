The Infinity Stones

Name Color Powers and capabilities Image
Soul Orange Perhaps the most dangerous of all, this stone has a mind and a hunger that only souls can satisfy. It allows its owner to steal, subdue and change the souls of the living or the dead. Adam Warlock was the sole custodian of this stone for many years and felt responsible for all the souls he had taken. Ironically, the stone is the gateway to an idyllic pocket universe.
Time Green The stone gives its owner full power over time. It makes the past, present and future accessible or visible. A master of the stone can use its power as a weapon, trapping enemies or even entire worlds in endless time loops. The stone can also cause objects and beings to physically revert to a young age or age.
Space Blue The stone allows its owner to exist in any surrounding space, whether it be space or underwater depths; move objects, yourself and other creatures to any corner of the universe; deform and rebuild space.
Mind Yellow Is the most destructive of all. The stone allows you to increase the power of consciousness of the owner and gain access to the thoughts and dreams of other beings. With the support of the Stone of Power, with its help it is possible to gain access to all existing minds at the same time. The stone can also release inner demons, whatever they may be in other universes, and drive them back.
Reality Red Is the only stone that was in a fluid state. It allows its owner to fulfill any desires, even if these desires are contrary to scientific laws. If used carelessly, the stone can lead to disaster. By touching the edge of reality, it causes catastrophic damage that can only be isolated by the power of the Space Stone, the Soul Stone, and the Power Stone.
Power Purple The stone has access to all the power and energy that has ever existed or exists in the future, nourishes other stones and enhances their effects. The stone allows the wearer to duplicate almost any physical superhuman ability and become invulnerable, and therefore invincible, using sheer strength alone.
